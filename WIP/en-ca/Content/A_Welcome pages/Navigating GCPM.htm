Navigating GCPM

Every page in GCPM has a navigation bar, tiles and icons:

Navigate to records using tiles and icons under the navigation bar.

Navigation buttons provide quick access to search, settings and other tools: 

Quick search

  • Search across multiple record types in one search
  • Be specific to narrow search results

Recently viewed items

  • Shows last 40 records and last 10 views, with most recent at top
  • Pin / unpin items appear at top of list

Quick add

  • Quickly create any record available in the display

Advanced find

  • Retrieve any type of record
  • Create a query, then click ! Results
  • Choose which columns to display and sort results
  • Export results to Excel

Settings

  • Personalize GCPM - choose preferred language (English or French)
  • Set up email signature, and others

Help

  • Links to GCPM online help.
  • May be context-specific for certain GCPM locations and records.
