Whether both conditions are true.

OR

Whether at least one of the conditions is true.

Wild cards. Cannot be used in the middle of a string.

Wild card characters must be escaped by square brackets, same with square brackets.

‘%[%][[]Hello[]][%]%’

Functions

Specifier

Description

Example

Result

LEN

Gets length of string.

LEN(‘Hello’)

5

ISNULL

If expression is null, use default value.

ISNULL(null, 0)

0

IIF

Gets one of two values depending on the result of a logical expression.

IIF(true, 1, 0)

1

TRIM

Removes all leading and trailing blank characters like \r, \n, \t, ' '.

TRIM(‘ Hello ‘)

Hello

SUBSTRING

Gets a substring of a specified length. Starts from 1.

SUBSTRING(‘Hello World’, 7, 5)

World
