About contribution agreements

When clauses and contribution agreements (CAs) are configured for GCPM, users can generate a formatted CA for an approved project with the click of a button. The documents can be stored in the document management repository. It may take a few moments to generate if a large number of fields are displayed in the document.

A contribution agreement can include:

  • Standard clauses, special conditions (editable by an officer) and conditional clauses.
    • Standard and conditional clauses are managed in a document template (see Document Generator, below).
    • Special conditions are managed with the Clauses feature.
  • Any data stored on the project and related entities can be included in the contribution agreement. This includes:
    • Performance indicators and targets
    • Project costs and cash flows
      For example, a yes / no question about advances can insert the relevant clause in the contribution agreement.
    • Financing
    • Assessment questions, and results
    • Account information (e.g. legal name, operating name). Contacts affiliated with the account can be identified in the CA as needed.
    • Key dates.

Once a project is approved, fields that have been copied in the agreement are locked. These can only be changed (unlocked) by making an amendment, to maintain data integrity.

Clause templates and document templates for contribution agreements are managed by a GCPM administrator.

Proactive disclosure: Once a contribution agreement is executed, GCPM can generate an open government data file, with all data required under current Proactive Disclosure rules (March 2019). Amendments can be monitored for material changes that could trigger a re-disclosure of amended data.

GCPM can automatically generate documents during transitions, and automatically store them in the document management repository. This requires the following: 

  • Documents are generated automatically based on XML templates. See About the document generator.
  • Your organization has integrated GCPM with its document repository. See About the document manager solution
  • Transitions, decisions and action sets are configured to generate and store documents in the repository. See About the transition framework.
Design decisions
Clause feature
  • The Clause feature is designed to manage clauses that require project-specific language. Most clauses are stored in a document template.
  • Your organization can leverage clause categories to increase the number of clauses managed with the clause feature instead of in a document template.
  • Your organization must develop and maintain at least two CA document templates: one for each official language.
Document Generator
  • Appropriate clauses are automatically displayed in the correct section of the CA, with appropriate numbering.
  • Microsoft word (v. 2016 or higher) supports XML. No additional plugins are required. Requires knowledge of XML programming to create output documents.
  • GCPM generates contribution agreements in Microsoft Word. Your organization can leverage tools to generate PDF copies, if desired.
  • GCPM does not include actions to auto-create a non-editable PDF. Your organization may choose to configure this differently.
  • The GCPM sample contribution agreement displays the project budget and cash flow in a grid in the CA Annex. Note, the tables in Word do not automatically sub-total amounts. You need to manually refresh the document to have the subtotals appear.
