Configure a risk template

Risk templates ensure the same risk categories and elements are copied to every project risk assessment in a program (or client risk assessment, if template is configured for client risk).

To configure a risk template: 

  1. In the program which you want to configure risk, navigate to Project Assessment Templates section.
    If there is no pre-configured template, or you wish to create a new one, click + to add a new record.
    Alternatively, browse to the Business Administration > Templates > Risk Template | A . Create a new record.
  2. Complete the fields, using onscreen help and the notes below: 
    • Template type: Choose the type of template.
      • Client - the template will be available for client risk assessment.
      • Project - the template will available for project risk assessment.
    • Template status: Options are (Under construction / completed). Changing the status to completed locks fields on the template.
      • Completed templates are available to associate with a program, or for GC Client risk assessments.
      • Under construction = All fields can be modified by a configurator. The template is not available for creating project or client risk assessments.
    • Scoring type: Choose the how the risk score is summarized, based on the risk score. These summaries may be text (high-medium-low) or numeric (1-2-3-4-5). Common options are available, others can be created. See About scoring
    • Scoring rollup: Choose the appropriate formula to calculate a score for categories in this template.
      The formula chosen provides a numeric value that is a summary calculation of risk category scores.
  3. Open the Risk Category section, and click + to add a new risk template category.
    Use the grid or form option - these screen shots use the form option.
  4. Complete the fields for the risk template category, using onscreen help and the notes below: 

    • Name: provide a name (English and French). Officers will see these names.
    • Risk template: Pre-populated. This is the template associated with this risk category
    • Risk category: Choose a risk category to use in this template.
      This field displays all Risk Categories | A already defined in your organization. If you choose an existing category, the risk rollup and weight fields will display the category values once the record is saved. Alternatively, you can create a new risk category.
    • Score rollup: this option determines how overall risk scoring will be calculated.
      See About scoring for further details.
    • Weight: (optional) Provide a weight of 1, or other value if you choose to weight the category differently.
    • Risk element type: Choose the grid to display this risk category.
  5. Choose risk template elements.
    • Name: provide a name (English and French). Officers will see these names.

    • Risk template: pre-populated. This is the template associated with this risk category

    • Risk category: Choose the risk category this element is associated with.
    • Risk element: Choose the risk element from the drop-down associated to this risk template element, or create a new risk element.
    • Weight: (optional) Provide a weight of 1, or other value if you choose to weight the category differently.
    • Risk element type: Choose the grid to display the risk element.
  6. Save and close the record.
