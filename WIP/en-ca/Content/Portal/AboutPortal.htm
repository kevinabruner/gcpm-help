About the portal

The portal is a SharePoint website which acts as an internet-facing front end to GCPM. It is a tool for communication between an RDA and internet users, as an alternative to phone, email, and paper forms. The portal allows your organization to collect information submitted on the portal by clients, process the submissions in GCPM, and publish the results back to the portal for client review. Clients can monitor the status of their submissions and projects with your organization.

Communications

The portal supports three types of communications:

  • Secure Messaging between the client and your organization. A more secure alternative to email, where the contents of the message remains private on the portal and in GCPM.
  • Form submissions: Applications, claims, advances, and performance reports. New forms can be added to the portal. Forms submitted on the portal automatically trigger business processes in GCPM (e.g. applications). The form engine for the portal is Alphinat SmartGuide.
  • Shared documents. Arbitrary documents may be uploaded to the portal to share with the RDA. These documents are automatically copied to GCPM.

Zones

The portal supports three zones. All of the communications listed above are available in each zone, although not every form will be available in every zone.

  • Personal Zone: Anyone on the internet can register an account on the portal. Each user is assigned to his / her own Personal Zone. The primary purpose of the personal zone is secure communications with the RDA. This allows a user to start doing business with their RDA. Available forms include applications.
  • Client Zone. A client is a legal entity (not an individual) who does business with your organization. Users can be invited to a Client Zone in various roles (read, edit, admin). A user can review summary information about their client, and review the status of any applications for the client. The Client Zone also contains links to any projects which have been published to the portal (see Project Zone). Admin users can invite other portal users to join their client. Invited users may be able to read and edit documents, secure messages, and submit forms for a client and its projects. Available forms include applications.
  • Project Zone: A Project Zone represents a unique contribution agreement between a client and your organization. It displays financial information for the project (such as budget information, or repayment schedule for the project). A Client Zone may contain multiple Project Zones. Available forms include claims, advances, and performance reports.

Any communications submitted from a zone becomes associated with that zone in GCPM.

For example: a secure message sent from a Client Zone will show up under that client in GCPM. A claim submitted from a project will become a claim for that specific project.
