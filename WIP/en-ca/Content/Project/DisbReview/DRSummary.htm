Disbursement summary

A disbursement review summary displays fields used to calculate the payment, including any adjustments.

Note: before recommending a payment, the reviewer should ensure that manual adjustments are displayed in the summary section (e.g. hold back, PAYE) to prevent overpayment to the recipient.

  • Automated calculations are displayed but locked. Onscreen help provides details on these fields:
    • Adjusted payment amount: The payment amount that has been calculated based on the review of the claim.
    • Other adjustments: The calculated total of any changes made in the “other adjustments” grid in the review section of this disbursement
    • New advance: The calculated total of any advances made in the “advance” grid in the review section of this disbursement
    • Cumulative deferred: The calculated total of any amount that has been marked for deferral in the review section of this disbursement
    • PAYE reduction: The amount to be deducted from the PAYE total for this project
    • Approved payment: The amount to be paid to the client; includes any advances, over payments or hold back changes made to the adjusted payment amount.
  • Fields that can be adjusted, and will update the approved payment include: 
    • Advance settlement: The amount of the claim that is applied to advances from prior disbursements
    • Advance settlement override rationale: If the reviewer overrides the advance settlement, a rationale must be provided.
    • Overpayment: The amount to be collected from the client based on a previous overpayment. An amount in this field will reduce the total approved payment amount.
    • Apply hold back: Indicate if a hold back should be applied to this payment. : If a percentage for hold back is identified, the system will calculate the amount of the hold back automatically if Apply hold back is set to YES. If there is no hold back %, or the officer wants to apply a different amount, the amount can be entered in the field.
    • Hold back: Enter an amount to hold back from this payment.
    • Release a hold back: Enter an amount to release a hold back for this payment.
    • Advances outstanding: The total amount of outstanding advances from previous disbursements that have not been accounted for by a claim.
    • Deferrals outstanding: The total amount of deferred items from previous disbursements that have not bee released.
    • Hold back %: The percentage that should be held back on the disbursement, if YES is selected for applying a hold back.
    • Net disbursed + pending %: The total percentage of payments made to date on the project, including the current review. The total should be below 100%. If the % is above 100%, the project is over paid based on the department cash flow and needs to be adjusted with an amendment.
    • PAYE: When a PAYE is selected, the payment to the PAYE is automatic. Amounts applied to the PAYE will automatically reduce the free balance in the PAYE.
      Note: Only PAYEs that have been set up and approved can be displayed for selection.
