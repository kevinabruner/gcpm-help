Register PAYE on a project

PAYE (Payable at Year End) records document a PAYE in the financial system.

A PAYE registered on a project displays the fiscal year and amount available. The PAYE is displayed on a disbursement review. As costs are allocated to the PAYE, and funds are paid to the GC client, the expended and remaining amounts displays on the PAYE.

To register a PAYE for a project:

  1. In a disbursement review, navigate to the PAYE field. Click +NEW to create a PAYE.

  2. Complete the fields on the PAYE:

    • Fiscal year: Choose the fiscal year the payment is taken from.
    • Amount: Enter the amount of the PAYE. Disbursements taken from the PAYE will reduce the amount available.
    • Pending reduction: (calculated field) The amount of prior year costs to be applied to the PAYE amount.
    • Pending balance: (calculated field) The balance available in the PAYE before the pending reduction costs are applied.
    • Actual reduction: (calculated field) Is this amount filled in once the disbursement review is paid.
    • Actual balance: (calculated field) Is this amount filled in once the disbursement review is paid.
  3. Save and close the record.

Note: 

  • The PAYE reduction (calculated field) in the disbursement review indicates the approved payment that can be applied to the PAYE for the fiscal year.
