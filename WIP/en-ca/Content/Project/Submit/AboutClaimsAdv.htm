About claims and advance requests

Claims and advance requests are submitted by clients to receive payment for project-related expenditures. GCPM stores each claim and request as separate records to permit flexible claim management. Multiple claims and advance requests can be combined into one disbursement review and payment. Alternatively, several claims and advances submitted at one time can be handled by more than one disbursement review and payment. Note, to be configured locally based on your organization's options in financial integration.

  • Claims describe costs incurred by the client. Cost items are displayed in a grid, and include cost category, description, supplier, invoice date, amount and HST / GST. Other fields may be configured to meet your organizational requirements.
  • Advance requests describe what an advance will be expended on. Advance items are displayed in a grid, and include the cost category, supplier, and expected cash flow requirement. Other fields may be configured to meet your organizational requirements.

Clients can submit claims and advance requests either online, or manually. Online submissions are transferred to GCPM, according to portal configuration settings, and a snapshot of the data is captured in a non-editable PDF. The PDF is stored in a location accessible to the client as well as staff at your organization.

Configuring claims and advance forms for GCPM

Claims and advance request forms must be configured separately in GCPM and on a portal. When a client "Submits" an advance request online, data is transferred to GCPM via web services.
Note, to be configured locally based on your organization's options in financial integration.

The GCPM forms contain these data: 

  • Administrative information such as the type of submission, client data, date submitted, associated program, etc. This data can be used to set up administrative dashboards for claim / advance / application receipt and registration, and workload management.
  • Unique ID: generated automatically, provides a common reference to both the client and your staff to ensure correct identification of an application.
  • Entity-specifc data Claims forms contain cost items, advance requests contain advance items.
Configuring forms for the portal

There are several configuration tasks related to the portal: 

  • Design portal forms using SmartGuide Designer software. Create static or dynamic portal forms.
  • Map portal fields to GCPM fields.
  • Modify or build your own web services to transfer data from the portal to GCPM.

Note: 

  • Claims / advance requests submitted online have the status draft in GCPM.
  • Claims / advance requests submitted manually, must be input manually. These records have the status draft in GCPM, until manually changed to completed.
  • Only claims / advance requests that are completed in GCPM will trigger the Disbursement Review business process flow.
Automation
  • Minimal auto-population of claim information is currently configured. Your organization should determine what information is displayed on forms, and map this to the associated GCPM feature (e.g. claim, advance request, application, etc.).
  • Both claims and advance requests are auto-populated with data received from portal advance requests according to how the portal has been configured for your organization.
  • Claims, advance requests, applications etc. can be auto-populated with recipient data (e.g. legal name).
  • Use the Project ID to auto-register claims and advance requests to a specific project.
  • Your organization should consider how to manage claim requests submitted manually
