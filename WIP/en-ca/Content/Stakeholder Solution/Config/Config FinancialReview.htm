Configure financial review

GCPM includes a feature where the user can conduct a financial comparison of the GC client’s financial statements. The comparison is based on the current year, 2 previous years and 2 projection years. Information from financial statements can be loaded from existing data captured under the GC Client account or input during the comparison.

Key tasks to configure financial review:

  1. The Financial Review entity has a separate form for the financial statements of for-profit and non-profit entities. Validate that each form collects the information required to review financial statements at your organization. The form has five sections, additional fields can be added to meet the needs of your organization.
    Note: apart from calculated fields, there are no business rules in the financial statement form.
  2. The forms are configured to automatically match the type of client reviewed. For example, the for-profit form appears when doing a financial review of a for-profit organization.
  3. For each form, verify the options in the Financial Review Status field (egcs_financialreviewstatus) are valid for your organization. 
    The default options are [Under review / Review completed].

    Note: The financial review form is configured to display up to 5 financial statements in a single view. All fields in the financial statement forms are viewed in the financial review form.
