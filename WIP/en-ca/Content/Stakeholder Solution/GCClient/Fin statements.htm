Enter financial statements

Financial statements provides an important understanding of an organization's financial health. This is particularly important with for-profit companies that have repayable contributions. Financial statements can be entered in the GC Client record, or as an item in a monitoring activity.

To add a financial statement for a GC Client:

  1. In a GC Client record, navigate to Financial Statements.
    Any financial statements that have been added to GCPM are listed in the grid.

    Note: for-profit and non-profit entities have separate forms to enter financial statements, reflecting the differences in these types of financial statements.
  2. Complete the fields that describe the client's fiscal year and related data:
    • Type of statement required: Choose an option from the list.
    • Fiscal year end (month): Enter the month in which the client's fiscal year ends. For example, April = 4.
    • Fiscal year end (day): Enter the date in which the client's fiscal year ends. For example, April 30th = 30
    • Fiscal year end (MM/DD): A locked field - auto-populated based on the month and date provided.
    • Financial review frequency: Choose the frequency to review the financial statements for this client.
  3. Add a financial statement in the grid using the +New button.
  4. Complete the fields using onscreen help and the notes below as a guide.
    • Statement period: Enter the number of months covered by this statement.
    • Fiscal year end: A locked field - displays the fiscal year end.
    • Fiscal year: Enter the date of the fiscal year. If the statement covers two years (e.g. 2018-19), enter the latest year.
    • Type of financial statement: Choose the type of financial statement. These options are configured in a picklist for this field.
    • As at date: Indicate the date the statement was prepared.
    • Adjusted: Indicate if the statement has been adjusted.
    • Satisfies reporting requirements: Indicate if this statement meets the reporting requirements for this client.
  5. Complete the remaining financial statement using onscreen help as a guide.
    The fields used in your organization may be tailored to your clients' needs.
  6. Save and close the record.
