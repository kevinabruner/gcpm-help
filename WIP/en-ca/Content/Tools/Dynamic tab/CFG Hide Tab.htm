Hide form tabs in business process flows

You can configure form tabs to be hidden at different stages of a business process flow. This is a useful way to tailor user experience to show only relevant form sections at a given stage of a business process flow.

Hide form tabs

To configure form tabs to hide during a BPF: 

  1. Navigate to Configuration > Hide Tabs and click CFG Hide Tab.
  2. Create a new CFG Hide Tab record, and fill in the fields using onscreen help and the notes below.
    • Business process flow: Choose from the business process flows (BPFs) available.
      Note: the stages in the BPF display along the top row of the table.
    • Form: Choose a form available from the dropdown list.
      Note: All of the forms for the entities included in the selected business process flow are displayed in the dropdown list. Each tab of the selected form displays in the first column of the table below.
  3. To hide a tab during a stage, remove the check in the appropriate check box. If the box is checked, the tab will display.
    Tip: Choose the first check box in the row to display a form tab in all stages of the business process flow.
  4. Click Save Configuration. A floating window displays a confirmation message.

  5. To test the configuration, enter a record and change the stages by clicking on them . The tabs should automatically hide or show based on the configuration.

Display forms in a business process flow

To configure a form to display in a BPF:

  1. Open the selected form in designer mode.

  2. Add the egcs_hidetab_initialization.js web resource to the form and call the init function on the form’s OnLoad event (if it is not already configured that way).

  3. Save and close the form, and publish your changes.

The form should now display according to your settings when the business process flow is initiated.
