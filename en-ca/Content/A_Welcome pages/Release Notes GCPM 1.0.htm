Release Notes GCPM 1.0

Release Date: June 2018 (last updated: June 26, 2018)

Be sure to also review the Dynamics 365 Release notes*.

Introducing GCPM Release 1.0!

GCPM Release 1.0 is a stakeholder management solution built on the foundation of Dynamics 365. Dynamics 365 has many stakeholder management features, which you can learn about through online learning resources and training courses.

Some GCPM features are hidden from the business user security role; these can be modified by the local system administrator. Features that are hidden by default in R1 are not supported by the GCPM team.

Key Dynamics 365 features include:
  • Account hierarchy*
  • Activities*
  • Connections and Roles*
  • Dashboards and charts*
  • Duplicate detection*
  • Marketing lists*
  • Reports*

GCPM features are highlighted below, with links to the online help. Your feedback is welcome! Send to: kathy.mortimer@canada.ca.

Summary of Release 1.0 GCPM Features

Key features for Accounts

  • Bank accounts: store direct deposit and withdrawal account numbers on the account profile - always accessible to staff with appropriate access rights.
  • Accreditations: store licensing, certificates, and other accreditation information on the account profile.
  • Activities* in the social pane include meetings, email and phone messages. The "Info Request" is a custom activity used to track client responses to information requests.
  • Use connections and roles : connections and roles are available in R1, but watch for changes and additional features for connecting accounts in future releases.
  • About duplicate detection rules: duplicate detection can be customized to meet your organization's requirements. Several reports and options tailored to GCPM are included. See below for known issues.
  • Employment numbers: store employment related information for accounts
    • Addresses: stores primary and mailing address linked to the business number, as well as additional addresses such as "head office" or "regional office."
    • Latitude / longitude on primary address
    • Geocode field on primary address
  • Add an account tag to an account Tags identify accounts with shared attributes. You can tag all accounts in "clean tech" or "advanced manufacturing"
  •  : The business number provides access to a set of data that can be transferred to GCPM. The business identity on the Account profile includes: 
    • Account names - the business name as registered on the incorporating documents, and the operating name, in English and French. A separate field stores an acronym, as needed. Also includes historical changes to the legal name.
    • - indicate if the business identity information has been validated (either manually or through a web service). Once validated, the business identity fields are locked, and can only be unlocked by users with appropriate security roles.
    • - jurisdiction of incorporation, date, certificate number and relevant legislation
    • Legal state: tracks if the account is active, bankrupt, or other legal states.
  • NAICS codes: Both primary and additional NAICS can be added to the account record.
  • Promote to GC client: an additional set of information is created for accounts that have applied for funding, or other G&C related business (e.g. co-sign an application).
    • In Release 1.0, a simplified G&C Account includes credit checks.
    • In future releases, additional features will include tracking financial statements, display project history, client risk assessment, and others.
