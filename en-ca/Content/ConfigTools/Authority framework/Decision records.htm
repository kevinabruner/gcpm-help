Decision records

Decision records are configured to capture these key data for a decision: 

  • Name of the decision (EN and FR)
  • Owner (the person who made the decision, or the delegate)
  • Status ?
  • Decision type: 
  • Decision date: date the decision was made
  • On behalf of: if the decision was delegated, identifies the person meant to make the decision)
  • Rationale: if any comment or reasoning is provided by the decision maker, it is tracked here.
  • Related documents: any documents that were automatically created as part of the decision, or manually added by the decision maker, appear here.

Entities that have decisions:

  • Advances
  • Amendments
  • Claims
    ClaimGC Clients submit claims to receive funds for costs incurred for a project. decision record (update screenshot)
  • Disbursements
  • PAYEs
  • Projects

    • Project decision record - Note screenshot must be updated
