Assess project risk

  1. Click the Risk Assessments icon in a project site map.
    The view displays risk assessments done to date for this project.

  2. Create a new risk assessment by choosing + .
    The risk assessment form opens. Fill in the fields using onscreen help and the notes below.
General
    • Many of these fields are locked and auto-populated.

    • Risk template: 

    • Review date:
    • GC Client monitoring:
    • Date completed: inserts the date when the Complete risk assessment button is clicked.
    Assessment

  • Scoring unchanged: Indicate Yes if the risk rating has changed since the last risk assessment. Indicate No if there has been no change.

    Calculated score: The automatically calculated score, based on the responses to the risk questions / elements. If the score is not auto-calculated, provide a score in the next field.

    Revised score: An alternative score entered by the officer, if the officer feels the auto-calculated score is not an accurate portrayal of the project risk.

    Rationale: If an alternative score is provided, a rationale is required.

    Schedule future assessment: Indicate yes to schedule a future risk assessment date.

    • Risk Category: If risk elements are not used, you can assign values to the risk categories.

    Risk Element: If risk elements are used, you can assign values to the elements. The category values, and overall risk value, auto-calculates.

    1. Save and close the risk assessment. You can return to a risk assessment at a later time.
    2. When the risk assessment is finalized, click the button COMPLETE RISK ASSESSMENT.
