Manually submit a claim

If clients submit a claim or advance request by email, mail, fax, etc., the data on the form must be submitted manually. the claim on behalf of the client.

Until then, create claims using the options below:

  1. In a project, verify the project is in a "disbursable" state.
  2.  Open the Disbursement tab.

    The Claims and Advance Requests grid appears in this tab.

  3. Click +New to create a new claim.

    The disbursement request form opens.

  4. Complete the fields in the General tab. - e.g. Received date, Review officer, type of claim.
    Fields that are auto-calculated include: request number, status, source, total costs incurred.

  5. Save the record.
  6. Open the ClaimGC Clients submit claims to receive funds for costs incurred for a project. tab and complete the date fields. In the Costs Incurred grid, click + New
  7.  Add costs incurred (claim items). Complete the fields using the onscreen help, and the notes below.
    The screenshot below uses the Form option.

  8. Continue adding claim items until completed.
  9. Click Transition to submit the claim.
    The claim becomes read-only when submitted. This uses the "Lock" feature.
